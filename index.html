Google Find My Device

For Android phones - FREE and works best

How to use:
  1. Go to: findmymobile.google.com
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Select your device - See location, ring, or lock

Apple Find My (iPhone)

For iPhone, iPad, Mac

How to use:
  1. Go to: icloud.com/find

  3. See location, play sound, or erase

Call Your Carrier

Your mobile carrier can help with cell tower data

What to do:
  1. Call your carrier's customer service
  2. Verify you're the account holder
  3. Ask them to track the phone or suspend service
Carrier Hotlines (US):
  • AT&T: 1-800-331-0500
  • Verizon: 1-800-922-0204
  • T-Mobile: 1-800-937-8997

If Phone is Stolen

  • Change all passwords immediately
  • Enable Lost Mode to lock device
  • Report to police with IMEI number
  • Contact carrier to suspend service

IMEI: Dial *#06# on any phone
